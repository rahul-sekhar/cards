This is a collection of drawings on old visiting cards I made between Feb and April, 2014. It began as an experiment on a bus ride, where I wanted to see how strangers would respond to being asked to draw something. It shifted to friends and people I met while travelling.

I'd give someone a card with a small shape, line or squiggle drawn on it and ask them to complete it while trying to use up all the space on the card.

People often asked what the point was and whether I was conducting some sort of experiment. There really is not much point to it. I wanted to see how people responded, and to overcome barriers I had to approaching strangers. It ended up being a kind of repository of memories.

The idea fizzled out of course, but there are some beautiful drawings, so I'm trying to keep this as a record.
